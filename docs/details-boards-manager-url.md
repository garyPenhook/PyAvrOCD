<details>
  <summary><b>How to enter a new boards manager URL</b></summary>
<p></p>
  <p>First you need to open the <code>Preferences</code> dialog, which you find either in the <code>Arduino IDE</code> or the <code>File</code> menu. </p>
<p align="center">
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/felias-fogg/pyavrocd/refs/heads/main/docs/pics/preferences.png" width="30%">
</p>
<p>
Next you have to open the field <code>Additional boards manager URLs</code>.
</p>
<p align="center">
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/felias-fogg/pyavrocd/refs/heads/main/docs/pics/preferences-dialog.png" width="50%">
</p>
<p>
Now type in the package index URL.
</p>
<p align="center">
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/felias-fogg/pyavrocd/refs/heads/main/docs/pics/additional-board-urls-xmini.png" width="50%">
</p>
<p>
Finish the dialog by clicking on two <code>OK</code>  buttons in succession.
</p>
</details>
